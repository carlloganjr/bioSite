LAURA Interests About Home

Laura's favorite things to do consist of reading, writing, and hiking. These all seem to flow together as one can lead to another. When she makes time to read, her favorite author, Fredrik Backman, captures the nuances of human nature on every page. Poetry is another enjoyable reading activity for Laura and has also led her to appreciate spoken word poetry. As well as enjoying poetry and the spoken word, Laura writes in both genres as an outlet for her creativity. Her spoken word poetry has led to performances across the United States, and she hopes to perform again in the future. She has also written for newspapers and magazines throughout her career.

Aside from reading and writing, Laura enjoys traveling and hiking. She has hiked in many places throughout the nation, enjoying the mountains of Utah the most. Travel has presented her with a chance to try new things on a continual basis. Her favorite places to travel are mostly the National Parks since they were great destinations to experience wonderful adventures. She has been to half of the National Parks, of which there are 63, and plans to see the remainder. She has also been to numerous National Monuments, Memorials, and Forests along the way. Her favorite National Monument is Craters of the Moon where she went spelunking for the first time. The experience made her feel empowered when she overcame her fear of being in a dark cave with no way out. She kept going in the cave and eventually came to an end and the exit, which was an incredible accomplishment for her. When Laura has finished a day of reading, writing, hiking, traveling, and spelunking she loves to sit down with a cup of tea to relax for the rest of the day.
